Nose of a Thousand Winds is a game created by the UTS Playmakers Dev Team in 2022.
You play as Schnoz Swanson, a friendly little anthropomorphic nose who needs to explore and fill out his collection!
Climb, bounce, and sneeze your way through two open-ended levels! Explore peaks far above the clouds and a virus infested country town. Use the level select in the pause menu to go between levels as you please.
Use your allergens to your advantage to traverse the world by the propulsion of your own sneezes.

I was one of the lead programmers on the project, both working on significant portions of the code, and assigning work to the other programmers on the team, while ensuring smooth integration of new systems! It is definitely the largest game I had ever worked on at the time, and continues to be one of the larger projects that I have participated in. The game is currently available to play at its Itch.io page.
