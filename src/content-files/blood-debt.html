Blood Debt is a game that I worked on as part of a team for Game Design Studio 1 at UTS. It was selected to be presented at the 2023 UTS Games Showcase, and won the MSI Creativity Award! We were also invited to showcase our game at the SXSW Student Showcase! I worked on the procedural level generation, level 3D modelling, UI, and all of the sound design and music for the game.

Battle your way through the Arena! Can you make it to the end? Or will you suffer a gruesome fate?!
As you delve deeper, you will face many threats such as the sneaky Swindlers and the unrelenting Cyber Canines. Make your way through the arena and find secret rooms that feature additional Vendors and Health Vats, and take down the Boss!
The game is currently available to play at its Itch.io page.
