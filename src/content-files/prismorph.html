Prismorph is one of my favourite games that I have created. It was created for the 2021 UTS Playmakers Adaptive Jam using Unity. It is a topdown game where the player fights of a constant stream of enemies by absorbing their powers and using them against the enemies. It was awarded 1st place in the jam, marking the second game jame at UTS I would win! It can be downloaded at its Itch.io page.
