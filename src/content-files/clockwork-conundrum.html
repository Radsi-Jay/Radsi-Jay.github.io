Clockwork Conundrum is a game that I worked on as part of a team for Introduction to Computer Games Development at UTS. It was selected to be presented at the 2022 UTS Games Showcase, and won the award for best Intro to Games Design project! I did the majority of the programming for the game, as well as creating some of the art, sound effects and music.

The game is a timelooping topdown 2D puzzle, where you must use your past selves to help you escape. Make sure they don't see you, lest the timeline collapse into a paradox! So far Clockwork Conundrum is the best game I have created (or at least that's what I think), and I am currently enjoying developing it further! The game is currently available to play at its Itch.io page.
