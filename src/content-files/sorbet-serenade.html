Sorbet Serenade is a game I made in 2 days for a game jam! The aim of the game is to listen/watch the customer's order, then recreate the order to the same musical beat! I collaborated with a friend (Clai) who made all the art assets at really short notice, while I did everything else for the game! Definitely one of my favourite game jam games that I've made!
