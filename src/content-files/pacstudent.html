Pacstudent: Ocular Onslaught was created as my final project for Introduction to Computer Games Development at UTS, where we had to recreate Pacman with the given guidelines, as well as adding our own twist! I decided to give Pacman the ability to teleport, and also had quite a lot of fun with the visuals of the game. I got full marks for my project, and it was selected for the FEIT Student Prototype Exhibition (Spring 2021). It is available for download at its Itch.io page.
