Duck Duality is a game that I created with a team of three other students for a game jam hosted after Introduction to Games Development. It is a 2D rougelike platformer, where the player moves through a series of rooms by defeating enemies. At the end of each room the player is shown an 'upgrade module', which provides a buff and a debuff. They can choose to take the module or keep their current ones, with a max of three modules. The game can be downloaded from its Itch.io page.
